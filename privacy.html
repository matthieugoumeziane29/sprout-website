Privacy Policy

Your privacy matters to us

Last Updated: January 2025

At Sprout, we take your privacy seriously. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our water tracking application.

1. Information We Collect

Information You Provide

  • Water Intake Data: The amounts of water you log in the app
  • Usage Data: Information about how you interact with the app, including your streaks and progress
  • Device Information: Basic device type and iOS version for compatibility purposes

Information Collected Automatically

  • App Analytics: We may collect anonymous analytics data to improve app performance and user experience
  • Crash Reports: Technical data if the app experiences errors or crashes

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide and maintain the app's core functionality
  • Track your hydration progress and plant growth
  • Send you hydration reminders (if you enable notifications)
  • Improve the app's performance and features
  • Understand how users interact with the app
  • Fix bugs and technical issues

3. Data Storage and Security

Your water tracking data is stored locally on your device. We implement appropriate security measures to protect your information, including:

  • Data encryption on your device
  • Secure data transmission protocols
  • Regular security assessments

4. Data Sharing and Third Parties

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share anonymized, aggregated data that cannot identify you individually for analytics and improvement purposes.

We may use third-party services for:

  • Analytics (e.g., to understand app usage patterns)
  • Crash reporting (e.g., to identify and fix bugs)
  • Push notifications (e.g., for hydration reminders)

These third parties are required to protect your data and use it only for the services they provide to us.

5. Your Rights and Choices

You have the right to:

  • Access Your Data: View all data stored by the app
  • Delete Your Data: Remove all your water tracking history from the app
  • Control Notifications: Enable or disable hydration reminders at any time
  • Opt Out of Analytics: Disable analytics data collection in your device settings

6. Children's Privacy

Sprout is intended for users of all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 without parental consent. If you believe we have collected information from a child under 13, please contact us immediately.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date. We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically.

8. International Users

Sprout is designed to work globally. If you use our app from outside your country of residence, please be aware that your data may be transferred to and processed in different jurisdictions.

9. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or our privacy practices, please contact us at:

Email: privacy@sproutapp.com

10. Your Consent

By using Sprout, you consent to this Privacy Policy and agree to its terms.
